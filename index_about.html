About Urban Sprouts

We are a plant and gift boutique located in historic downtown Renton, just south of Seattle. It's our mission to help you and your plants thrive!

We're happy to help you with any problem you might be having, whether that's finding the right plant for a gift or saving a plant that suddenly falls ill. Just call, text, or message us and we'll help you out! We also offer a variety of services including re-pots, local plant delivery, and plant diagnosis/treatment guides. You can always pick up a curbside pickup order that you've place online. Online orders are held behind the lobby desk - just show us your order confirmation email at the desk to claim your order!

biofertilizers are not fertilizers. They are directly used for supply of nutrition to crop plants. Microorganisms such as bacteria, fungi, and blue green algae are largely used as biofertilizers.
